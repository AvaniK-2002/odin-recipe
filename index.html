Recipe 2: rasgulla

very tasty and famous bengali recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 litre milk, full cream
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 5 cup water
  • 3 pod cardamom

Instructions:

  1. firstly, in a large vessel get 2 litre milk of milk to a boil stirring occasionally.
  2. once the milk comes to a boil, pour 2 tbsp lemon juice and stir well. you can alternatively use curd or vinegar.
  3. stir until the milk curdles keeping the flame on low to medium.
  4. squeeze off the water completely. be careful as the curdled milk will be very hot.
  5. rinse off the curdled milk with fresh water to remove sourness from lemon juice.
  6. mash the paneer till it turns out smooth texture without any grains of paneer.
  7. now prepare small ball sized paneer and keep aside. cover and keep to prevent from drying.
  8. now boil the water for 5 minutes.
  9. drop in rolled paneer balls one by one into boiling sugar water.
  10. finally, enjoy rasgulla chilled or as it is.

Recipe 2: masala maggi

very easy and fast recipe famous among youth

Ingredients:

  • 2 tsp oil
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ tomato, chopped
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 pack maggi noodles
  • 1 pack tastemaker
  • 2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped

Instructions:

  1. firstly, in pan heat 2 tsp oil and saute 2 clove garlic and ½ onion.
  2. add ½ carrot, 2 tbsp capsicum, ½ tomato and 2 tbsp sweet corn.
  3. saute until the vegetables turn crunchy.
  4. add 1 cup water and get to a boil.
  5. now bread 1 pack maggi noodles roughly and add 1 pack tastemaker.
  6. mix well and boil for 2 minutes or until the noodles are cooked well.
